SAMKALP



KHIT celebrates SAMKALP Every Year 13-14 September as a tribute to the greatest Indian Engineer Bharat Ratna, Sri Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya.
The main aim of the techno cultural fest "SAMKALP" is to bring together innovative students in various fields of engineering to a common platform and to explore the inherent skills and capabilities of the future technocrats. The fest will be held every year to make it an ideal platform for students to share views and experiences in the field of Engineering & Technology. The fest also provides awareness on various new technologies and opportunity to learn the recent developments in the technological front. The fest also conducts various events and games in addition to the above, to encourage the students in all directions.
